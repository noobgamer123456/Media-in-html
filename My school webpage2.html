Adamjee Cantonment Public School

About Us

Adamjee Cantonment Public School was established in 1960 and has grown to become one of the most prestigious educational institutions in Dhaka. The school is located in the heart of Dhaka Cantonment, providing a serene and disciplined environment conducive to academic excellence. With a strong emphasis on discipline, education, and character, the school has consistently produced exceptional students who contribute significantly to society.

Teachers

The school boasts a dedicated faculty of 267 highly skilled teachers who are experts in their respective fields. These educators are committed to nurturing the potential of each student, ensuring they achieve both academic and personal growth. Regular workshops and professional development programs help teachers stay updated with the latest teaching methodologies, fostering an innovative learning environment. The passion and dedication of the faculty are key to the school's continued success.

Students

Adamjee Cantonment Public School is home to a diverse student body of over 7,500 students enrolled in various classes, from pre-primary to higher secondary levels. The school emphasizes holistic development, encouraging students to excel in academics, sports, and cultural activities. Through numerous competitions and events, students showcase their talents, gaining recognition on both national and international levels. The sense of community among students fosters lifelong friendships and a spirit of collaboration.

Excellence

Excellence is a tradition at Adamjee Cantonment Public School. The school consistently achieves a 100% pass rate in board exams, with many students securing top positions. Alumni of the school have gone on to excel in prestigious universities and successful careers in various fields, including medicine, engineering, and the arts. Numerous awards and scholarships testify to the outstanding performance of students, and the school takes pride in its role as a launching pad for future leaders.

Fun Section (Troll)

Despite its focus on academics, Adamjee Cantonment Public School fosters a vibrant and enjoyable school culture. Students frequently engage in light-hearted moments, creating jokes and memes about their daily experiences. These shared moments of fun contribute to the school's unique atmosphere, making the journey of education both rewarding and memorable. As many students fondly say, "School life is the best time of your life."
